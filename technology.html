Technology - Talent Aggregators

The Technology industry in India in a short span has become one of the pillars for the economy. It has grown at a rampant pace and is excepted to continue the journey upwards. Today the industry contributes nearly 190 Billion USD and is expected to reach $350 Billion USD by 2025.

Industry Expertise

The Technology industry in India has come a long way since its inception. The journey that started with Software Exports in early 70s & 80s turned into a project driven software services market, making India a preferred outsourcing destination by the 90s wherein the US based companies started establishing there development teams benefiting from the low cost skilled talent availability in the country. The next wave came in the last decade with the country seeing loads of Product Development and R&D centers being setup in India. Digitization is driving this decade and going forward the emphasis will be largely upon Artificial Intelligence, Augmented Reality, Robotics, etc. The Technology team at Talent Aggregators has seen through this evolution of the Technology Industry since the last 2 decades and understands the challenges associated with it.
